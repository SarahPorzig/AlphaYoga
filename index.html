About Me

I am currently developing and enhancing my skills and expertise in the field of Front End Web Development, specifically focusing on HTML, CSS and JavaScript as well as plugins including jQuery and Bootstrap.

I enjoy the process of building the foundations of a website using HTML and then transforming the basic functionality into something beautiful. I particularly enjoy creating websites with simple user interfaces and seamless transitions.

I am passionate about the blending of design and technology. I enjoy being able to turn concepts into dynamic and exciting user interfaces and experiences.I find the world of technology inspiring and am excited to be involved in the field as the industry evolves.

Caramar Estate

A place to showcase all that is 'Caramar' Estate. The site theme and structure gives a soft, peaceful feel reminicent of the countryside. The application of whitespace is comfortable and pleasant for the eye, heightening the user experience.
