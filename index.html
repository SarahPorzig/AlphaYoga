Caramar Estate

HTML5 CSS3 JavaScript jQuery Bootstrap 3

A place to showcase all that is 'Caramar' Estate. 'Caramar' is located in South West Victoria near the Otways and is surrounded by native bush, inspiring the use of dark and lush forest green across this site. The site theme and structure gives a soft, peaceful feel reminicent of the countryside. The application of whitespace is comfortable and pleasant for the eye, heightening the user experience. A carousel central to the page displays scenic photography taken across the property. The site is visually appealing and easy to use across all platforms.
